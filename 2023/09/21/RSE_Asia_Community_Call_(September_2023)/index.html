RSE Asia Community Call (September 2023)

Sep 21, 2023

An opportunity for people who work with or manage (research) software to meet and discuss challenges and solutions, share skills, opportunities, and resources. Join this call if you: want to learn about research software engineering, develop software for research, mostly do coding or software developing in a research group, are a doctoral or postdoctoral researcher who mostly develops software, have build software while doing research, and/or want to join the emerging research software engineering community in Asia.
